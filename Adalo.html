ACCELERO Speed Gate Flap Barrier

ACCELERO Flap Barrier is designed to control high traffic pedestrian flow entering or exiting restricted areas. The Retractable flap design ensures a fast and efficient traffic throughput while preventing unauthorised personnel from entering and exiting the facility. Multi infra red beams work in tandems with a powerful micro processor to detect unauthorised access and tailgating. Access Control System can be easily integrated to form an intelligent security management system. The ACCELERO body is fabricated using brushed stainless steel with various designer top finishing creating an elegant entrance control which blend seamlessly into most door environment.

    Features :
  • Fail safe Design ensures retractable flaps return back into the housing to allow unblocked access in the event of power failure
  • Multiple infra red beams detect users passing through and prevents the flaps causing injury and damage
  • Hydraulic buffers and dampers built in to absorbs shocks and vibration to offer a fast and smooth operation
  • Unique lever design connecting the flaps to the motor ensures a long cycle opration and service life
  • Programmable settings allows users to choose multiple entry/exit settings. Normally Close, Normally Open, Emergency Close, Emergency Open, Fail-safe Auto Open
